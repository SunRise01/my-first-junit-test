Technology Solutions

Technology is vital to modern, digital businesses. It is constantly changing and businesses must change with it or miss out on important opportunities. Our clients trust our knowledge to find ways that keep them relevant, competitive, and empowered.

Industries

Fintech Cards & Payments Media & Entertainment Ticketing

To compete, our fintech clients continually need to provide new and better services. Since we have our own R&D center, Softjourn is uniquely positioned to help. We’re constantly looking for relevant ways to apply new technology that brings our clients growing needs to life.

It may be how to apply biometrics including voice, hand swiping to make payments, or the precise Blockchain to use for their needs. By researching new developments or creating our own, we’re better able to keep our clients competitive or even compel them to become the dominant player.

Fintech Solutions

We are a technology services provider with an emphasis on helping prepaid card service and program managers. As a domain expert, we understand prepaid, issuing cards for payroll services, travel and entertainment, or expense.

We’ve been working in prepaid since 2005, so no one has to explain such industry details as requesting advances, tracking detailed expenses, or concerns about fraudulent card transactions.

Which makes us invaluable partners for our clients. We can proactively recommend cutting-edge technology that more effectively handles fraudulent requests for advances, how to apply OCR tech that quickly categorize expenses, or using AI to red flag abnormal card usage – and much more.

Cards & Payments Solutions

From Live Video and Video on Demand, to Social Media Video Platforms and Live Events—we have extensive knowledge and experience in these areas.

We started to work on Media & Entertainment in 2007. Since then we’ve become a go-to provider known for our creative solutions. This has lead to our hosting many U.S. television series, live events and VOD, including The Daily Show, Jersey Shore, EPiX, MTV, Nickelodeon, Big Brother, and more.

Media & Entertainment Solutions

We’ve been working with ticketing service providers since 2004. Over the years we’ve acquired a unique understanding of ticketing that clients seek. With us, they don’t waste time explaining a box office app, why offline mode is important, or how users work. Which means we can get up to speed quicker and new ticketing service functionality gets to market faster.

Clients understand our expertise makes us an invaluable partner. We’re able to offer business-building insights like suggesting new functionality and advanced technology. So we help our clients do more than remain competitive, we give them the tools to go after new markets and more profits.
